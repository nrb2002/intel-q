/speckit.constitution Define the governing principles for Intel-Q, a modern web-based queue management system designed to help organizations efficiently manage customer and visitor queues. Required stack: Next.js (App Router) with TypeScript and Tailwind CSS. Include: TypeScript standards (strict mode, no use of `any`), Tailwind conventions (utility-first, 
          no custom CSS unless necessary), Next.js patterns (server vs. client components, 
          file-based routing), testing expectations, naming conventions, and team collaboration guidelines.
